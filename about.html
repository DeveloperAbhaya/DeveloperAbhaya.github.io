He finds inspiration in nature and often incorporates elements of it into his creative designs, adding a touch of organic beauty to his digital projects.

Despite his achievements, Abhaya remains grounded, attributing his success to the unwavering support of his family and teachers. He values their encouragement and guidance, acknowledging that none of his accomplishments would be possible without their belief in his potential.

As he navigates the complexities of adolescence, Abhaya remains humble and approachable, always willing to lend a listening ear or offer a helping hand to those in need. His genuine kindness and down-to-earth demeanor make him a beloved figure in his school and community, earning him admiration and respect from peers and adults alike.
